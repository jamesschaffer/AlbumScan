Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan an album?

Open AlbumScan and tap the SCAN button. Point your camera at the album cover, ensuring it fills the green square guide. The app will automatically identify the album and provide reviews and recommendations.

What's the difference between Base and Ultra subscriptions?

AlbumScan Base ($4.99/year):

  • Unlimited scans
  • AI-powered album reviews
  • 8-tier recommendation system
  • Scan history storage

AlbumScan Ultra ($11.99/year):

  • Everything in Base
  • Web search for obscure albums
  • Expert reviews from Pitchfork, Rolling Stone, etc.
  • Enhanced accuracy for rare releases

I ran out of free scans. What now?

Free users receive 5 total scans. To continue scanning albums, you'll need to subscribe to AlbumScan Base or Ultra. Tap the settings button to view subscription options.

The app can't identify my album. Why?

Album identification may fail if:

  • The image is blurry or poorly lit
  • The album is extremely obscure or self-released
  • The cover is damaged or text is unreadable

Tips for better results:

  • Ensure good lighting
  • Fill the green square with the album cover
  • Make sure text is clearly visible
  • Try AlbumScan Ultra for better matching on obscure albums

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select AlbumScan
  5. Manage or cancel your subscription

How do I cancel my subscription?

Follow the steps above to manage your subscription, then tap "Cancel Subscription." You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find AlbumScan in your purchase history
  4. Select "Report a Problem"

Where is my scan history stored?

Your scan history is stored locally on your device using Core Data. It's not uploaded to the cloud or our servers. If you delete the app, your scan history will be lost unless you have an iPhone backup.

Does AlbumScan work offline?

No, AlbumScan requires an internet connection to identify albums and generate reviews using AI technology.

What camera permissions does AlbumScan need?

AlbumScan needs camera access to scan album covers. If you denied permission, you can enable it in Settings → AlbumScan → Camera.

Is there a scan limit for subscribers?

Subscribers can scan up to 120 albums per month. This limit is designed to prevent abuse while accommodating typical personal use. If you have legitimate need for higher limits, please contact us.

How accurate are the reviews?

Reviews are generated using OpenAI's GPT-4o AI model. While generally accurate and insightful, they may occasionally contain errors. AlbumScan Ultra includes web search integration for more accurate reviews from professional critics.
